# Limbe.Net Theme Variant Guide

Two upload-ready theme variants are included in `dist/`.

## Limbe.Net Coastwave

ZIP: `dist/limbenet-coastwave.zip`

Best for a bright tourism-magazine feel focused on beaches, ocean travel, Limbe, Kribi, Mount Cameroon, and ticket/tour conversion.

Visual mood:

- Electric ocean teal
- Coral orange
- Sunny yellow
- Lush emerald
- High-energy coastal hero image

Recommended when the homepage should feel bold, sunny, and immediately travel-focused.

## Limbe.Net Festival Trail

ZIP: `dist/limbenet-festivaltrail.zip`

Best for a colorful destination-guide feel focused on culture, crafts, road trips, highlands, food, festivals, and broader Cameroon discovery.

Visual mood:

- Royal blue
- Magenta
- Saffron yellow
- Emerald green
- Culture and highland hero image

Recommended when the brand should feel richer, more editorial, and more culture-forward.

## My Recommendation

Start with `Limbe.Net Coastwave` for the public launch. It gives the strongest first impression for a tourism site and makes Limbe.Net feel immediately tied to Cameroon beaches, mountains, and trip planning.

Keep `Limbe.Net Festival Trail` as the alternate option if you want the site to feel more cultural, magazine-like, and nationwide from the first screen.
